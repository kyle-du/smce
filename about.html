ABOUT US

SMCE, headquartered in Silicon Valley, is a leading High-Tech Enterprise specializing in the development and manufacturing of DC micro motors, coreless DC motors, and both brushed and brushless DC motors. Our product offerings span a range of sizes from 3.2mm to 65mm, with over 100 different models available for customers to choose from. With many years of experience in the DC motor industry, our products can be widely applied in fields such as aerospace modeling, precision electronics, automated robotics, medical and beauty equipment, and other high-precision industries. Our commitment to quality and innovation has earned us recognition from customers around the globe.

IQC


IQC serves as the first critical checkpoint for product quality before production begins. Its primary goal is to identify quality issues at the earliest stage, shifting quality control upstream to reduce costs. Allowing defective materials to enter the production process can result in defective workflows or finished products, ultimately affecting the quality of the company's final offerings.



Our factory is equipped with a comprehensive range of testing instruments specifically designed for motors, including a coordinate measuring machine (CMM), motor lifespan tester, high and low-temperature tester, salt spray test chambers, and anechoic chambers. Additionally, we utilize precision instruments such as surface roughness testers, rotor comprehensive testers, and roundness testers to ensure the highest quality standards.
